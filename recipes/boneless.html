Description

The pizza is one of the weirdest dishes around the world

Ingredients

  • Unbleached all-purpose flour
  • Oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Combine flour, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, paprika, and garlic powder in a large bowl. Whisk together egg and milk in a small bowl
  2. Dip each piece of chicken in egg mixture, then roll in flour mixture. Repeat so that each piece of chicken is double-coated. Refrigerate breaded chicken for 20 minutes
  3. Fry chicken in hot oil in batches. Cook until golden brown and juices run clear, 5 to 6 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
  4. Combine hot sauce and butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat sauce in the microwave until melted, 20 to 30 seconds. Pour sauce over fried chicken; mix to coat
